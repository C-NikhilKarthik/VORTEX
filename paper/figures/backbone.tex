\caption{Shared feature extraction backbone common to all three system
configurations. Incoming frames are converted to grayscale, processed through
FAST-based keypoint detection, binary descriptor computation, and
Hamming-distance matching. The architecture is cleanly divided into a frontend
feature processing pipeline and a backend motion estimation solver, where
correspondences feed either a homography solver (global alignment) or a
Lucas--Kanade tracker (frame-to-frame update) to yield the final pose estimate.}
